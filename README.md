## Overview

UBPIS (Universal Business Profit Intelligence System) is a comprehensive data intelligence and analytics platform designed to transform raw business data into actionable insights. It provides a complete ecosystem for business analytics, forecasting, and decision-making through an intuitive interface and advanced AI-driven features.

This system is ideal for businesses, analysts, and decision-makers who want to visualize, explore, and predict business outcomes efficiently.

---

## Features

- **Data Upload & Exploration**  
  Upload datasets in various formats (CSV, Excel) and instantly explore data with schema, types, and preview.  

- **Visual Analytics & Dashboards**  
  Multiple interactive visualizations (charts, KPIs, tables) powered by Plotly and Streamlit to understand trends and patterns.  

- **Data Cleaning & Missing Value Handling**  
  Built-in utilities to handle missing values, duplicates, and data inconsistencies.  

- **Forecasting & Predictive Analytics**  
  Use advanced machine learning models to forecast trends, predict KPIs, and generate actionable business insights.  

- **AI Assistant**  
  Ask questions about your data or predictions and get intelligent answers using integrated AI features.  

- **Download Insights**  
  Export processed datasets, reports, and visualizations for presentations or further analysis.

---

## Tech Stack

- **Python**: Core programming language  
- **Streamlit**: Front-end interactive dashboard  
- **Pandas & NumPy**: Data processing and manipulation  
- **Plotly**: Advanced visualizations  
- **Scikit-learn**: Machine learning & predictive analytics  
- **OpenAI API**: AI-powered assistant for data insights  
- **Openpyxl**: Excel file support  
- **Python-dateutil**: Date/time manipulation
